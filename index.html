Our Mission

Your Choice Medical, LLC is a new Concierge Internal Medicine Membership Practice that will change your primary care experiences to a personalized patient centered approach. It is our mission to provide comprehensive medical care for adult patients centered around accessibility, availability, convenience, choice and preventative care.  In order to provide this level of quality in healthcare we will substantially limit the number of accepted patients to our practice.

Accessibility 

Appointment within 24hrs during regular office hours with one of our providers.

Availability

Call Dr.Ficht’s or a nurse practitioner’s cellphone during off hours for important medical matters.

Convenience Telemedicine

HIPPA secure video conference appointments with a provider when you are unable to come in our office.

Choices

Patients can choose any specialist compatible with their insurance plan.  As an independent unaffiliated medical practice, it is our goal to give patients the choice to seek a local specialist or go elsewhere.

Preventative

Patient will be offered access to complimentary health experts to enhance their well being, such as a personal trainer, nutritionist, etc.
